Here is Huiguang Wang (王慧光). I am a current Master's degree candidate in the Department of Intelligent Construction at Hunan University. Currently, I serve at the Prof. Lu Deng's Group, advised by Prof. Lu Deng and Prof. Ran Cao.
I am passionate about robotics, familiar with various robotic arms and structured light cameras and aspire to alleviate the burden on workers through the utilization of robotic arms. Therefore, I mainly focus on robotic arm manipulation based on 6DoF pose estimation, which have been applied in rebar cage welding and tying.
If you are interested in any aspect of me, I would love to chat and collaborate, please email me at - whg0917@hnu.edu.cn

[Highlight] Recently, We have been paying our attention to an end-to-end 6DOF pose estimation network, which can be applied in bin-picking, AR, and robotic manipulation. If you were interested in our recent work, please join us without any hesitation!
