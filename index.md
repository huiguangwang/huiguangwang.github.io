<h2>Biography</h2>
<p><div style="text-align:justify"> I am currently a researcher of <a href="http://hsslab.com/">State Key Laboratory of High-End Server and Storage Technology</a>, Inspur Electronic Information Industry Co., Ltd. Before that, I worked in KE Holdings Inc. and CloudMinds Technologies. I obtained my Master and PhD degrees of Computer Graphics from Beijing Normal Universtiy in Jun 2013 and Jan 2018, supervised by <a href="https://ai.bnu.edu.cn/xygk/szdw/zgj/556.html">Prof. Zhongke Wu</a> and <a href="https://ai.bnu.edu.cn/xygk/szdw/zgj/563.html">Prof. Mingquan Zhou</a>, respectively.</p>

<p>My research interest includes 3D Vision related technologies, such as 3D shape generation, audio-driven 2D/3D face animation, 6D object pose estimation, point cloud registration and vision-based robotic grasping. In my spare time, I often write technical articals and put them on my CSDN blogs as well as my WeChat Subscription “3D视觉前沿”.</p>
